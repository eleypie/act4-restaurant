About Us

How it all began

BotaniQ was born out of a vision to elevate vegan dining beyond just an alternative—it’s an art form.
BotaniQ first started on a food truck, starting from humble roots before climbing in notoriety and soon reaching out to a separate market: fine dining.
Since opening, BotaniQ has been a gathering place for food lovers, professionals, and conscious diners alike—offering a menu that proves vegan cuisine can be just as refined, intricate, and satisfying as any gourmet experience, while maintaining affordability as connection to their roots.

Mission

“At BotaniQ, we are redefining plant-based dining by blending professional culinary expertise with fresh, sustainable ingredients. Our mission is to craft vegan dishes that are as refined, intricate, and exciting as fine dining, providing a sophisticated yet welcoming experience for those who crave bold flavors and conscious eating.”

Values

Culinary Excellence

We treat plant-based ingredients with the same respect and technique as traditional fine dining.

Sustainability & Ethics

Every choice we make, from sourcing local produce to minimizing food waste, reflects our commitment to the planet.

Innovation & Creativity

We constantly push the boundaries of what plant-based dining can be, experimenting with textures, flavors, and presentation.

Authenticity & Tradition

While we innovate, we honor the deep culinary roots of Southeast Asian cuisine, respecting time-honored techniques.

Guest Experience First

Every detail, from plating to ambiance, is designed to create a seamless and memorable dining experience.
