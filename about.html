About Us

How it all began

BotaniQ was born out of a vision to elevate vegan dining beyond just an alternative—it’s an art form. Our founder, a former fine-dining chef, traveled through Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, and Malaysia, inspired by the depth of flavors in plant-forward Southeast Asian cuisine. Seeing an opportunity to merge professional culinary techniques with sustainable dining, they created BotaniQ: a space where plant-based food is not just healthy, but bold, exciting, and exquisitely crafted.

Since opening, BotaniQ has been a gathering place for food lovers, professionals, and conscious diners alike—offering a menu that proves vegan cuisine can be just as refined, intricate, and satisfying as any gourmet experience.

Mission

At BotaniQ, we are redefining plant-based dining by blending professional culinary expertise with fresh, sustainable ingredients. Our mission is to craft vegan dishes that are as refined, intricate, and exciting as fine dining, providing a sophisticated yet welcoming experience for those who crave bold flavors and conscious eating.

Staff

Our team is made up of seasoned professional chefs, mixologists, and culinary innovators who share a passion for pushing the boundaries of plant-based cuisine. Every dish is crafted with precision, technique, and creativity, ensuring that each guest experiences the depth and artistry of vegan Southeast Asian flavors.

Values

  • Culinary Excellence – We treat plant-based ingredients with the same respect and technique as traditional fine dining.
  • Sustainability & Ethics – Every choice we make, from sourcing local produce to minimizing food waste, reflects our commitment to the planet.
  • Innovation & Creativity – We constantly push the boundaries of what plant-based dining can be, experimenting with textures, flavors, and presentation.
  • Authenticity & Tradition – While we innovate, we honor the deep culinary roots of Southeast Asian cuisine, respecting time-honored techniques.
  • Guest Experience First – Every detail, from plating to ambiance, is designed to create a seamless and memorable dining experience.
