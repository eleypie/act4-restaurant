The Story Behind

BotaniQ was born out of a vision to elevate vegan dining beyond just an alternative—it’s an art form. Our founder, a former fine-dining chef, traveled through Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, and Malaysia, inspired by the depth of flavors in plant-forward Southeast Asian cuisine.

BotaniQ has been a gathering place for food lovers, professionals, and conscious diners alike—offering a menu that proves vegan cuisine can be just as refined, intricate, and satisfying as any gourmet experience.
